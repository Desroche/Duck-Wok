Testimonials

I recently had the pleasure of dining at Duck Wok, and I must say, the experience was truly delightful. The atmosphere was warm and inviting, with a pleasant aroma of spices wafting through the air. To start, I ordered the keow krob, a traditional Thai chicken wrapped in a wonton crepe. The chicken was so fragrant and packed with flavor, with a perfect balance of sour and spicy notes.
- "Dave Mustang"

The sweet and sour chicken balls I recently tried at a Duck Wok were an interesting blend of sweet and savory flavors. The outer coating was crispy, providing a satisfying crunch with each bite. However, the sauce used to coat the chicken balls was a little too sweet for my taste, overpowering the subtle flavors of the chicken. Additionally, I found the sauce to be a bit too thick, which made the dish a bit heavy.
- "John Foodie Extrodinaire"
